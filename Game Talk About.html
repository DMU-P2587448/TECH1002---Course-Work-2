About Us

Game Talk is a passionate group of gamers dedicated to bringing you, our fellow players, the latest gaming news. But that's not all, we also want to show you that games have the power to connect people as part of a shared passion and how much fun there is in AAA productions and indie games. Join us today to receive the latest information about upcoming productions, the latest equipment and us, because Game Talk is not one person, Game Talk is You.
